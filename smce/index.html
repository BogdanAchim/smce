About SMCE GROUP T&D

Founded in Romania in 2008, we are a specialized engineering company providing international technical services in protection systems, transmission and distribution of electricity, and power generation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to fill the need for commissioning resources, often expressed by the leading profile companies and to support their project managers to complete their projects in a best possible way.

Our Approach

Mindful of the need to establish collaborations based on the freedom to work together in a mutually satisfactory way, we set up win-win relationships with our customers and our partners or employees.

Our Dream

Our dream is to create a large team of professionals, able to work together, to support each other, and lastly to be able to guide and grow, to share our knowledge with new generations of engineers.

Our Core Services

We provide comprehensive engineering and commissioning services for electrical power systems worldwide, specializing in protection systems, transmission & distribution, and power generation.

Engineering of Control & Protection Terminals

One of our core activities in the area of engineering is to provide relay or system configurations. We are able to deal with most of the common protection devices such as Siemens, ABB, AREVA, GE.

Line distance and differential protection
Differential protection of transformers
Busbar protection
Breaker management devices
Generator protections

Commissioning of Primary & Secondary Equipment

Our commissioning engineers are always aiming to reduce the risk of a project becoming delayed or running out of the budget. If we can find solutions, either on factory (during FAT), or on site (during SAT) we will implement them as fast as possible.

Field commissioning
Protection system commissioning
Commissioning of primary equipment

Site Management

Our experience allows us to take the responsibility of building the site on time, at the highest standards, following the design and contributing with our own know how. We had similar experience in few places in Africa, dealing with local man power, renting cranes, oil treatment machines, etc. When the instalation work is done, we can go ahead with the commissioning. One man from the beginning to the end, an ideal solution for slow going projects in difficult countries.
